What is SeeAround.me?

A web and mobile app to see and share hyperlocal news.

Anyone can create a post relating to a location - like a local event, new restaurant, yard sale, recent crime, etc.

Anyone nearby can then see that post, comment on it, and vote it up or down.

Local is important to us

This is Oakland-grown and we want to give back.

SeeAround.me is intended as a social utility with no current revenue model. However, we intend to offer this resource in support of local organizations. And, if we generate a profit in the future, will invest a substantial portion into the local community where this is first being incubated: Oakland.
