Post, comment, vote, message… to see and share what’s happening nearby in Pokémon Go!

All posts are location-based, and the app uses your location so you can see nearby posts (within the radius you set).

View posts on the Map or in List view. Comment and upvote good posts; ignore or downvote bad ones.

You can also search by keyword, like "Pikachu" or "Help," and filter by interests (keywords you can set in your profile), certain users you follow, most recent posts, etc.

If you’re in an area without any posts yet, you can lead that area and be the first to post!
